I DON'T BUY THIS / COMFORT OF VIOLENCE* a product of the first afterlife duty-free shop experience (Price on Demise)*

“I DON’T BUY THIS” (2022-forever) is a contemporary art store which explores what kind of consumerist goods people might purchase in the afterlife. The goal of this project is to start conversations about death and beliefs in relation to consumerism.
